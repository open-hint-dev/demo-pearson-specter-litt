# STARK INDUSTRIES VENTURES, L.P.

## Summary of Terms for Series B Preferred Stock Financing of Acme Corporation

**December 21, 2025 — Confidential**

This term sheet summarizes the principal terms of a proposed Series B financing of Acme Corporation (the "Company"). Except for the "No Shop" and "Confidentiality" sections, this term sheet is non-binding.

| Term | Summary |
| --- | --- |
| **Investor** | Stark Industries Ventures, L.P. ("Stark"), as lead investor |
| **Amount** | USD 30,000,000 |
| **Security** | Series B Preferred Stock |
| **Pre-money valuation** | USD 90,000,000, including an employee option pool increased to 15% of the post-money fully diluted capitalization |
| **Liquidation preference** | 1x the original purchase price, **participating**, capped at 3x |
| **Dividends** | 8% per annum, non-cumulative, when and as declared |
| **Anti-dilution** | Full ratchet for 18 months following closing; broad-based weighted average thereafter |
| **Conversion** | Convertible into Common Stock at any time; automatic on a qualified IPO of at least USD 250,000,000 |
| **Redemption** | At the election of holders of a majority of the Series B, at any time after the fifth anniversary of closing, in three annual installments |
| **Board of Directors** | Five members: two designated by Stark, two by the founders, one independent acceptable to Stark |
| **Protective provisions** | Customary, plus Stark consent for any annual budget, any debt over USD 1,000,000, and any hire with annual compensation over USD 350,000 |
| **Founder vesting** | 50% of each founder's vested shares re-subjected to vesting over 24 months from closing |
| **Pro rata rights** | All major investors; Stark additionally receives a super pro rata right of 1.5x in the next financing round |
| **Information rights** | Monthly management accounts within 15 days of month end; annual audited financials |
| **No shop** | 60 days from the date of this term sheet, binding |
| **Expenses** | The Company pays Stark's legal fees and expenses up to USD 75,000, payable at closing or upon termination of negotiations |
| **Governing law** | Delaware |

This term sheet expires if not countersigned by **January 9, 2026**.

*Stark Industries Ventures, L.P.*
